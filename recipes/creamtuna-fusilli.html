Cream-Tuna Fusilli

Get ready to taste heaven. Easy and delicious recipe you can put together in no time with only a few bucks.

Ingredients

  • a can of tuna
  • 200 grams of fusilli
  • a small carton of cream (150ml)
  • half an onion
  • half a green pepper
  • 2 cloves of garlic
  • salt
  • oregano
  • 500ml water
  • a spoon of salt

Steps

  1. Chop the garlic, green pepper and onion into little pieces
  2. Boil the water
  3. Stir fry the vegetables in a pan with a little bit of oil
  4. Put the fusilli into the water once it is boiling hot and add some salt
  5. Add the can of tuna to the veggie mix and then add some cream
  6. Cook the past for 9 minutes
  7. Once the tuna and cream are ready, add some oregano
  8. Strain the pasta and mix it with the tuna
  9. Enjoy!
