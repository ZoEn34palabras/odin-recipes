Cream-tuna Fusilli

Get ready to taste heaven

Easy and delicious recipe you can put together in no time with only a few ingredients

Ingredients

  • a can of tuna
  • 200 grams of fusilli
  • a small carton of cream (150ml)
  • half an onion
  • half a green pepper
  • 2 cloves of garlic
  • salt
  • oregano
  • 500ml water
  • a spoon of salt
